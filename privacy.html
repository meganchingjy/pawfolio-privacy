Pawfolio Privacy Policy 🐾

Last updated: 14 April 2026

This policy explains what personal data Pawfolio collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user. We have written it in plain English because we believe you deserve to understand exactly what happens with your data.

1. Who We Are

Pawfolio is a pet health records application. You can contact us at contact@pawfolio.sg with any questions about this policy or how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Pawfolio:

  • Account information: Your email address, used to create and manage your account.
  • Pet profile information: Your pet's name, species, breed, age, and other profile details you choose to provide.
  • Health records: Lab test results, diagnoses, medications, supplements, treatment courses, and veterinary visit notes that you enter into the app.

We do not store uploaded documents — only the information you manually extract and input from them. We do not collect payment information, government identification, or any health information about you as an individual.

3. How We Use Your Data and Who We Share It With

We use your data solely to provide Pawfolio's features. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

AI lab result explainer: To power this feature, we send the following information to Anthropic's API: your pet's species, breed, and age; lab test results and markers; active medications, supplements, diagnoses, and treatment courses; and relevant veterinary visit notes. This data is processed by Anthropic to generate plain-English explanations of your pet's lab results. Under Anthropic's API usage terms, data submitted via the API is not used to train their models. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Data storage: Your health records are stored using Supabase, a cloud database provider whose servers are located in Singapore (AWS ap-southeast-1 region). Streamlit Cloud, which hosts Pawfolio, processes application requests on its own infrastructure. We take reasonable steps to ensure these providers maintain appropriate data protection standards, including reviewing their data processing agreements.

No other third parties have access to your personal data.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your Pawfolio account is active.

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app via Settings. Deletion is immediate and permanent. We will also process deletion requests submitted by email at contact@pawfolio.sg within 30 days.

We do not retain copies of data sent to Anthropic's API — that data is processed in real time to generate explanations and is not stored by Pawfolio beyond what appears in your account.

5. Data Security

Pawfolio protects your data using the following measures:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS.
  • Your data is stored in a secured cloud database with row-level security enabled, meaning each user can only access their own data.
  • Access to Pawfolio's backend systems is restricted to authorised application connections only.

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, contact us immediately at contact@pawfolio.sg.

6. Your Rights

As a Pawfolio user, you have the right to:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you by contacting us at contact@pawfolio.sg.
  • Correct your data: Update inaccurate information directly in the app, or contact us to request a correction.
  • Delete your data: Delete your account and all associated data immediately via Settings within the app, or contact us at contact@pawfolio.sg and we will process your request within 30 days.
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for AI-powered features at any time by stopping use of the lab result explainer. This does not affect data already processed.

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you can contact the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) at pdpc.gov.sg.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you by email at the address associated with your account and update the date at the top of this policy. Continued use of Pawfolio after changes are notified constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
